Anna Karenina
was published in 1878 by
Leo Tolstoy.
It was translated from Russian in 1901 by
Constance Garnett.

Project Gutenberg

HathiTrust Digital Library.

John Singer Sargent.

League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
